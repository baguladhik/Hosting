Superior Customer Support

24/7 Dedicated Support: A responsive, knowledgeable support team available around the clock via phone, chat, or email can be invaluable to clients, especially those less tech-savvy.

Personalized Assistance: If you offer personal account managers or a concierge setup service, this can simplify onboarding and make troubleshooting easy for users.

High Uptime Guarantee

Reliability and Redundancy: Offering a 99.9% or even 100% uptime guarantee with infrastructure that includes redundancy across multiple data centers can be a huge differentiator, ensuring that clients’ sites stay live and accessible.

Enhanced Security Features

Security Beyond Basics: Provide robust, proactive protection, such as daily malware scans, DDoS protection, SSL certificates, and regular security updates. Extra layers of protection, like two-factor authentication and automated backups, give clients peace of mind.

Compliance for Sensitive Data: If you support PCI compliance for eCommerce sites or HIPAA compliance for medical sites, it’s a strong value proposition for specific sectors.

Flexible, Scalable Hosting Plans

Easily Scalable Solutions: Offering flexibility to scale from basic shared hosting to VPS or dedicated servers with minimal hassle lets businesses grow with you, avoiding any need to switch hosts.

Customizable Packages: Creating packages that clients can tailor with add-ons (extra storage, advanced security, or enhanced support) can meet the varied needs of different users without them paying for features they don’t need.

Optimized for Speed and Performance

Fast Load Times: By using optimized infrastructure, such as SSD storage, CDN integration, and caching, you can ensure your clients’ websites load quickly, which is crucial for both user experience and SEO.

Global Data Centers: A network of strategically located data centers ensures faster access times, especially important for global or region-specific websites.

Ease of Use with Cutting-Edge Technology

User-Friendly Control Panel: A well-designed, intuitive control panel (like a customized cPanel or a proprietary solution) makes website management accessible for all users.

Developer-Friendly Tools: Offering features like one-click installs, staging environments, Git integration, and SSH access can appeal to developers and agencies who need flexibility.

Commitment to Sustainability

Green Hosting Options: If your data centers use renewable energy, carbon offsets, or energy-efficient practices, it appeals to environmentally conscious clients and can be a unique selling point.

Transparent Pricing with Great Value

No Hidden Fees: Being upfront about costs and renewal rates builds trust with clients and helps avoid surprises down the line.

Affordable Plans with Quality Features: Offering competitively priced plans with value-added features (e.g., free domains, SSL certificates, email accounts) can attract a wide variety of customers.

Education and Community Engagement

Resources and Tutorials: Providing a knowledge base, blog, video tutorials, and webinars on everything from website setup to digital marketing can position you as a trusted partner.

Active User Community: Fostering a community forum or offering exclusive webinars and Q&A sessions can help clients connect and learn.

Proactive Monitoring and Maintenance

Automated Backups and Monitoring: Offering regular backups and proactive monitoring of server performance minimizes the chance of data loss or downtime, reassuring clients that their sites are always in safe hands.

Our full story

They say, necessity is the mother of invention. Well, EtherAuthority came into being, more or less in the same fashion. EtherAuthority has its roots in a web development company, that started way back in 2006. The parent company offered to create websites for businesses, at a time, when the concept of 'Going Online' was pretty nascent.

Over the years, clients begun to realise, having a website was not just about getting a website designed and developed. It begun way before that and extended way beyond that. They were faced with the challenge of reliable hosting. And so, there was this newly created demand - Why don't you do it all?

Thus began the laying down of initial fibres of hosting. LIttle by little, one server after another, a lot of pitfalls, grilling frustration, learnings, in short, evolution, Host IT Smart grew in number and nuances. Today, we have an entire fleet of servers, all set up locally, a team of nerdy experts that we could not have imagined functioning without, and over 5000+ happy clients.

How did it all become this plush? We like to give the credit to our support team. From the day we attended to our first grievance, till date, the team has not let us down. Even now, we make the same age old promise - we use the latest tech stuff and offer round the clock support. So no matter how tiny the glitch, no matter how short the rectification time margin, we still make it a point to listen and attend to each one of those problems.
